About Me

Hi! I'am Evichiru or you can call me Evie. I'm a comic artist, 2D artist, 2D Game Assets Artist, and a Character Designer. I’m passionate about bringing stories to life through illustration, comics, and digital design.


I love creating vibrant characters and immersive worlds that captivate audiences. My goal is to combine my artistic skills with storytelling to create engaging and memorable experiences. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work!
